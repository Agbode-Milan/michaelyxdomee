About me

With 8 years of experience as a skilled graphic designer and video editor, Michael Yao Xorla Domee has collaborated with a diverse range of companies, creating high-quality print and digital solutions. His expertise lies in delivering unique and effective visual designs that drive brand recognition and enhance customer engagement. Each project is approached with a tailored strategy to meet the specific needs of the client, ensuring impactful results every time.

As a creative thinker who is innovative and highly organized with a keen sense of color, Michael Yao Xorla Domee excels at working within brand and design guidelines while continually exploring new techniques in the design world. He is an effective communicator with a strong ability to collaborate seamlessly with team members. Michael is a graduate of IPMC Ghana, specializing in Graphic and Web Design, and NAFTI, specializing in Film Editing.

Due to his expertise, Mr. Domee has collaborated with top-notch firms, including Cluster Smash Solutions (Aug 2012 – Nov 2014) and Impact Digiprint (Jan 2015 – Dec 2015). He is now the CEO of Vision Effects (since April 2017) and the co-founder and creative director of United Vision Multimedia (since 2018).
